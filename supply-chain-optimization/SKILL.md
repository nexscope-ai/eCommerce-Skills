# Supply Chain Optimization

Analyze and optimize your entire e-commerce supply chain — from sourcing to customer delivery. Identify bottlenecks, reduce costs, and improve efficiency across all operations.

## What it does

**Input:** Current supply chain details, costs, and performance metrics  
**Output:** Comprehensive optimization plan with cost savings, efficiency improvements, and implementation roadmap  
**Use case:** Reducing operational costs, improving delivery times, scaling operations, solving supply chain bottlenecks

## Instructions

I'll conduct a complete supply chain audit and provide actionable optimization recommendations.

### Supply Chain Assessment

Share details about your current operations:

1. **Sourcing & Procurement:**
   - Supplier locations and lead times
   - Order quantities and frequency  
   - Quality control processes
   - Backup supplier arrangements

2. **Inventory Management:**
   - Storage locations (home, warehouse, 3PL)
   - Inventory turnover rates
   - Stockout frequency
   - Carrying costs and storage fees

3. **Order Fulfillment:**
   - Processing time from order to ship
   - Shipping methods and carriers
   - Peak season capacity
   - Return processing workflow

4. **Cost Structure:**
   - Product costs and payment terms
   - Shipping and logistics costs
   - Storage and handling fees
   - Labor and overhead costs

### The Optimization Process

1. **Current State Mapping:** Visualize your entire supply chain flow
2. **Bottleneck Identification:** Pinpoint constraints limiting performance
3. **Cost Analysis:** Break down costs by supply chain segment  
4. **Efficiency Gaps:** Compare performance to industry benchmarks
5. **Optimization Scenarios:** Model improvements with ROI projections
6. **Implementation Roadmap:** Prioritized action plan with timelines
